About Us

A little bit more insight about who we are

Catering Cave is a catering service that are always ready to help you plan your event and give the best quality food possible. We are working together with a lot of trusted vendors to make sure that your events have the best possible food experience. After all, we believe that there is no good event without good food.

We take care of all the details, from preparation to clean up. Our prices are disclosed up front in your event contract, and we do not impose hidden charges. Our team of highly qualified professionals will make any event a pleasure. From weddings to cocktail parties, or anything in between, Blusage Catering is your best choice.

Our food reflects our commitment to exceptional quality. All meals are prepared under the guidance of our trusted and licensed food investigator. Our menu items are prepared with a passion for perfection, and your guests will be raving about the wonderful food for days to come. We serve varieties of food that will suit whatever theme you have in mind for your event!
